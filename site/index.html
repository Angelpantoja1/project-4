    • Welcome to our restaurant

    We have been here since 1995, happily serving the town of Manteca Chinese food as a family run restaurant. We are located on the corner of Lathrop and Union Rd in the Raley's shopping center. Open everyday from 11am-9pm. We also deliver to anywhere in Manteca and Lathrop. Our lunch specials go on from 11am-3pm, and dinner specials run all day. Have a party you need catering for? Let us know, and we give discounts for party trays.
